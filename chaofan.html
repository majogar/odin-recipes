ChaoFan 炒飯

Chao Fan, which means “fried rice” in Chinese, has a rich history dating back centuries. It originated in China and has since spread worldwide. Chao Fan is a Chinese-inspired Filipino fried rice, with various simple ingredients.
Now Lets start cooking

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Chicken
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Canola Oil
  • Rice
  • Peas
  • Carrots

Preparation

  1. Heat oil on medium heat and add the beaten egg. Cook the egg and shred it into medium-sized pieces. Remove the cooked egg and add in the remaining oil.
  2. Cook the meat- using the same pan, add oil and cook it until it is no longer pink.
  3. Add the oyster and soy sauce to season. Add more or less, according to your liking.
  4. Add the frozen peas and carrots. Mix and add the rice. Add the egg.
  5. Add the cooked egg and add a dash of Roasted Sesame Oil.
The final stage

Your homemade Chao Fan is now ready to be savored. Garnish it with sliced green onions and a drizzle of additional soy sauce if desired.
